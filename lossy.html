What is Lossy Image Compression Algorithm?

A type of image compression that reduces file size by permanently deteriorating some image data that is less significant, such as redundant information. Even though the data loss is usually not notable from human eyes, the strength of the degradation depends on the strength of the compression, which may show the data loss to human eyes. This is why this method is also known as ‘irreversible compression’.
